Professional-Grade Auto Detailing

Expert care and craftsmanship for every vehicle.

Welcome to Lawson Detail Co., where professional-grade auto detailing meets meticulous care. Based in Charlotte, NC, we specialize in mobile and in-studio services tailored for private vehicle owners, fleets, and luxury car enthusiasts. Our commitment is to deliver consistently high-quality results through proven processes and expert craftsmanship—because your vehicle deserves nothing less.

What We Do at Lawson Detail Co.

Routine Exterior Detail

A thorough wash and sealant application designed to protect and enhance your vehicle’s paint, wheels, and tires.

Full Interior Detail

Complete interior cleaning including vacuuming, steam cleaning, and leather or fabric treatment—restoring your cabin to like-new condition.

Paint Correction & Gloss Restoration

Multi-stage polishing to remove swirls, scratches, and haze, restoring the paint’s clarity and shine.

Ceramic Coating

Long-lasting protection with premium ceramic coatings, including full prep and correction for superior durability and gloss.

Fleet & Corporate Vehicle Care

Tailored maintenance plans for fleets, with scheduled service, detailed reporting, and professional management.
